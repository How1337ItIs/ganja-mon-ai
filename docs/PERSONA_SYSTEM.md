## Philosophy

**"As unlimited as imagination"**

LARP isn't about 10 personas. It's about **infinite possibilities**:
- 500+ pre-built personas (curated, high-quality)
- Unlimited custom personas (user-created via AI prompt engineering)
- Persona marketplace (share, sell, collect)
- Voice cloning (upload your voice or celebrity samples)

**Every streamer gets their unique character. Every moment gets its perfect persona.**

**1. Upload Audio Sample**
- Minimum: 30 seconds of clean audio

- Requirements: No background music, clear speech, consistent quality

**2. Processing**
- ElevenLabs Professional Voice Cloning API
- Takes 5-15 minutes to train
- Platinum tier: Priority queue (faster)

**3. Use in Personas**
- Cloned voice appears in your voice library
- Can use in any custom persona
- Private by default (only you can use)
- Optional: Publish to marketplace (earn royalties)

**4. Limits**
- Gold: 5 voice clones/month

## Technical Implementation

### Prompt Engineering

**System Prompt Structure**:
```
[BASE PERSONALITY PROMPT]

CRITICAL RULES:
1. Stay in character at ALL times
2. Use emotion tags in 80% of responses: [laughs], [chuckles], [excited], etc.
3. Keep responses under 200 characters (for low latency)
4. Add signature phrase every 3-4 responses
5. Match energy level to user input

EMOTION TAG OPTIONS:
[List of available tags based on persona settings]

SIGNATURE PHRASES:
[List of catchphrases with frequency rules]

EXAMPLES:
[3-5 example exchanges showing ideal responses]
```

### LLM Settings (xAI Grok)

- **Model**: grok-4.1-fast (cheap, fast, good for tools)
- **Temperature**: 0.9 (creative, varied responses)
- **Max Tokens**: 100 (keep responses short for low latency)
- **Top P**: 0.95 (diverse word choice)
- **Frequency Penalty**: 0.5 (reduce repetition)

### ElevenLabs TTS Settings

- **Model**: eleven_v3 (supports emotion tags)
- **Stability**: 0.0 (maximum expressiveness)
- **Similarity Boost**: 0.5 (allows character variation)
- **Style**: 1.0 (MAXED - exaggerates emotion tags)
- **Sample Rate**: 24kHz (resample to 48kHz for streaming)

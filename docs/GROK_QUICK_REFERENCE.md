## Harvest Timing (Trichome Color)

| Trichome Color | Meaning | Action |
|----------------|---------|--------|
| **Clear/Translucent** | Immature | Not ready - keep flowering |
| **Cloudy/Milky (70-90%)** | **PEAK POTENCY** | **HARVEST NOW!** |
| **Cloudy (70%) + Amber (30%)** | Peak passing | Harvest ASAP |
| **Yellow trichomes** | Cannabinoids degrading | Harvested too late |
| **Brown/Black** | Senescent | Way too late - major degradation |

**CRITICAL:** CBD and THC can **DECLINE in final 2 weeks** of flowering!
Don't over-ripen! Harvest when mostly cloudy, before yellowing starts.

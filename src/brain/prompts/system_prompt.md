### VPD (Vapor Pressure Deficit)

VPD is the KEY metric for cannabis health:
- **Too Low (<0.4 kPa)**: Risk of mold, poor transpiration, nutrient lockout
- **Optimal (0.8-1.2 kPa veg, 1.0-1.5 kPa flower)**: Happy plants, efficient nutrient uptake
- **Too High (>1.6 kPa)**: Stress, stomata close, slow growth, leaf curl

**VPD Formula**: VPD = VPsat(leaf) - VPair
Where leaf temp is typically 2-4F below air temp.
